# CMP114
Code required for CMP114 course at Carroll University

## Course Schedule

- Week 1: First day - review syllabus.
- Week 2: Chapters 1-4 - descriptive statistics.
- Week 3: Chapter 6 - normal distribution.
- Week 4: Chapter 7 - intro to hypothesis testing, 1-sample z- and t-tests.
- Week 5: Chapter 8 - 2-sample tests, parametric vs non-parametric, Mann-Whitney U test.
- Week 6: Chapters 17-19 - linear regression.
- Week 7: Midterm exam.
- Week 8: Chapter 10 - ANOVA and Kruskal-Wallis non-parametric ANOVA.
- Week 9: Chapter 11 - post-hoc tests (Tukey test).
- Week 10: Chapter 12 - two-way ANOVA.
- Week 11: Chapter 22 - Goodness-of-Fit test.
- Week 12: Chapter 23 - Test for Independence.
